Introduction


55716 represent the number of accidents inside Metropolitan France in 2018. This means that on average, a collision between either two vehicles or a vehicle and a pedestrian occurred every thirty minutes .

As such, the risk of car traffic accidents is pretty frequent and sometimes deadly : From ONISR, 3248 people died on the Metropolitan road in 2018

The goal here is to zoom on the scope of those accidents and determine the factors of deadly collisions : while the highest cause of death is speed, I'd like to analyze the conditions that led to such tragedies. Maybe the driver was in a hurry ? Maybe the road was steep, slippery or snowy ?

Another goal is to determine how we can improve the vehicle to reduce collision damage and human casualties. This information may prove insightful to automobile constructors.

The dataset used is the set of all car accidents happening in 2018 from data.gouv.fr website. The dataset is composed of 4 .csv files :

  • caracteristiques-2018.csv : overview of accident (date-lat-lon)
  • lieux-2018.csv : about location situation
  • vehicules-2018.csv : about vehicle information
  • usagers.csv : about persons involved

Map of all the car accidents in Metropolitan France


1 - User analysis


We want to evaluate the relationship of three features :
  1. The age
  2. The sex
  3. The motive of trip
We'd like to perform analysis on two dimensions : first among all victims regardless of the gravity of accident, then on deceased victims only to spot differences.

From this barchart, we can see that the majority of victims are male and born around 1990s-2000s

From this barchart, most of the victims's motive were leisure. Second most frequent motive is work

Now let us perform analysis among only deceased victims to compare how our features evolve

A vast majority of deceased remain male but proportion of victims are much older

Most of the deadly accidents are due to leisure trip or going to work.

Drivers are the most vulnerable when they are distracted or stressed by the destination, regardless of the age or the sex

They may be too focused on reaching their destination and less focused of the trip itself



2 - Location analysis


The goal is to map which areas of France are the most dangerous by taking into account :
  1. The daylight
  2. The road category
  3. The slope of the road
  4. The weather


Deadly accidents occur mostly on district roads where there less signs and narrow lines

Accidents gravity are not impacted by specific weather, road surface or ambient luminosity



3 - Vehicle analysis


We want to determine which side of the vehicle is impacted first in deadly accidents

More than 60% of fatal accidents are due to frontal collision

Conclusion


The evil recipe for an unfortunate car accident is :

  1. Driver too focused on it's destination and not pying attention to the road itself
  2. Driving in narrow road with irregular amounts of curves, slopes.
  3. Full frontal collision where the driver isthe most vulnerable
It's nothing new, but this project has a goal to bring much more awareness to this subject
